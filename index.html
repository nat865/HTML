Freycinet National Park

Freycinet is one of the more "famous" crags due to it's picturesque atmosphere, all the toilet facilities you could ever need, a **super** short approach from the campsite and free camping! There is also tons of great hiking and the famous Wine Glass Bay lookout for your rest days! Click here to find out more about Freycinet National Park.
