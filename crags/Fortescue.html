Fortescue Bay

Where to stay

There is paid camping shared with LOADS of tourists. Be sure to pre-book MONTHs in advance if you want a spot.


Toiletries/Facilities

Very upper class toilets, hot showers, undercover picnic areas with bbq's and tap water (although please be conservative and bring your own water)


Weather

It was incredibly windy when we went. And given you are climbing sea stacks the wind factor is ramped up by 10x
